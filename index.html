Mission

Our goal is to provide trained and experienced advisors to help international business executives and managers to be more effective leaders.

Values
Operate Ethically
Focus on Relationships
Exceed Expectations

Is your workforce performing up to expectations? Are your managers achieving the goals you have set? If so, congratulations.

What if your managers could consistently meet or exceed goals? Would your company be more profitable? Would you have better customer satisfaction?

We provide tailored programs to help business executives and managers become more effective leaders. Our foreign relations expertise is a great fit for international business.

While building upon leadership basics, we focus on how regional and country-specific culture influences the way we apply leadership techniques.

Our Services

Assessments

Through observations and interviews, we can describe the leadership styles your managers employ and help you understand their impact on your company’s productivity. We can offer recommendations that would improve your output / profitability.

Profession Development Packages

We will tailor training packages to fit your needs. For international businesses, it could be useful to focus on your executives and managers, but include modules to help your employees understand and agree with the changes you will implement.

Country Studies

These are ideal for companies that want to expand their international presence. Through our extensive network, we can create a study to help you understand the business and security environment of a particular country.

Transition Management

Change can be challenging. We can be with you to help drive change. We’ll gladly provide follow-on assistance and assessments. We can also help you find people to temporarily or permanently fill key roles in managing change.
